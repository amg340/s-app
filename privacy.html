Privacy Policy

At SiloVPN, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect or store the following sensitive data:

  • Browsing activity and history
  • Connection logs or session durations
  • Original or assigned IP addresses
  • DNS queries or destination traffic
  • Personal traffic data content
We operate under a strict no-logs policy.

2. Limited Information We May Collect

To maintain and improve the service, we may collect minimal technical data:

  • Basic app diagnostics (such as crash reports)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is non-identifiable and used only for technical improvements to ensure SiloVPN works perfectly on all devices.

3. Use of Information

Collected data is used only to improve app performance, fix bugs, and maintain service stability.

Strict Guarantee: We do NOT sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

4. Security

We use industry-standard strong encryption and secure protocols to protect your data. However, as an engineering reality, no system is completely secure, but we strive for maximum protection.

5. Third-Party Services

SiloVPN may use limited third-party services (such as analytics or hosting providers) to ensure smooth operation. These partners have their own privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected directly inside the app to keep you informed.
