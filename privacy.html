Privacy Policy

At SiloVPN, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. By using SiloVPN, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Use of VpnService

SiloVPN utilizes the Android VpnService API to create a secure, encrypted tunnel for your internet traffic. This functionality is essential for providing core VPN features and is used solely for the purpose of securing your connection.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Strict No-Logs Policy: We do NOT collect, store, or track the following:

  • Browsing activity or websites visited.
  • Connection timestamps or session durations.
  • Original or assigned IP addresses.
  • DNS queries or destination traffic content.

3. Personal Data

SiloVPN does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. We believe in absolute anonymity.

4. Limited Technical Data

To maintain and improve service quality, we may collect minimal technical data such as:

  • Basic app diagnostics (e.g., crash reports).
  • Device type and operating system version.

This data is anonymized, non-identifiable, and used strictly for technical performance enhancements.

5. Use of Information

Any collected data is used strictly to improve application performance, fix bugs, and maintain service stability.

Zero-Sharing: We do NOT sell, trade, or share user data with any third parties.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect user data and ensure all communications remain safe and private.

7. Data Deletion & Contact

If you have any questions or wish to request data deletion, you can contact our support team at:

support@silovpn.me

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected directly within the application.
