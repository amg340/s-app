About SiloVPN SiloVPN is a lightweight and privacy-focused VPN application designed to provide secure and fast internet access. The app is built using the WireGuard® protocol, a modern VPN technology known for: - High performance - Strong encryption - Simplicity and efficiency WireGuard is an open-source protocol widely trusted in the security community. Our Mission SiloVPN aims to: - Protect your online privacy - Secure your connection on public networks - Provide a simple and user-friendly VPN experience We believe in transparency, minimal data collection, and giving users control over their privacy.
