Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using SiloVPN?

Simply download the app from Google Play, open it, and tap the central shield button. The app will automatically configure the best server using the WireGuard® protocol.

Is my data really private?

Yes. We operate under a strict no-logs policy, meaning we don't know who you are or what you do online. Your data is encrypted at the device level.

I'm experiencing slow speeds. What should I do?

Try switching to a different server location or restarting the app. Ensure you have a stable local internet connection for the best performance.

Can I use SiloVPN on multiple devices?

SiloVPN is currently optimized for Android devices. Stay tuned for future updates across other platforms.
